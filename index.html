Welcome

Fattoria La Torre, wine, restaurant, room rental in San Gimignano Siena Italy

La Torre farm takes its name from the antique10th Century Medieval tower, which has been skilfully refurbished and even today is the distinguishing feature of the rural village, La Villa, located in the hills of San Gimignano, in the centre of the most suggestive and fascinating landscapes on earth: a land of vineyards and olive groves.

This three hundred and fifty hectare farmstead produces some well known DOC and DOCG wines, including Vernaccia di San Gimignano, Chianti Colli Senesi, Chianti Colli Senesi Reserve, and San Gimignano Red.
And then there is the excellent extra-virgin olive oil, obtained entirely with the product resulting from the very first pressing of the drupe (the ripened olives), which features a fragrant aroma and a full-bodied and fruity flavour, to satisfy even the most demanding palates, making it ideal for healthy and natural food preparation. The wines made by La Torre farm are still produced according to traditional criteria and methods.
The rigorous selection of high quality raw materials along with support from the most modern manufacturing processes ensure goodness, a guaranteed quality and genuine products.
